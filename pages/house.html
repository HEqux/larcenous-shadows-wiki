HOUSE

OVERVIEW

"House" is the first level in Larcenous Shadows. In this level you need to collect at least $5000 whilst being chased by the Homeowner. In this level you need $5000 to leave, 7500 for 2 stars, and 10000 for 3 stars, 13000 for all items

COLLECTABLES:

  • 1 Stars (to leave): $5000
  • 2 Stars: $7500
  • 3 Stars: $10000
  • All Collectables: $13000
